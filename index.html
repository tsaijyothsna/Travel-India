Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal is one of the wonders in the World and famous tourists places located in Agra,India.It is built by Shah Jahan in the 17th century.


Hampi

Hampi, a temple town in northern Karnataka, was once the capital of the historical Vijayanagara Dynasty. This quaint place, has been receiving tourists from far off for ages.


Red Fort

The Red sandstone walls of the massive Red Fort (Lal Qila) rise 33-m above the clamour of Old Delhi as a reminder of the magnificent power and pomp of the Mughal emperors.


Ajanta Ellora Caves

Ajanta and Ellora caves, considered to be one of the finest examples of ancient rock-cut caves, are located near Aurangabad in Maharashtra, India..
